About Us

In India, medical emergencies are often complicated by a critical information gap at the point of care. First responders and emergency room staff frequently treat unconscious, disoriented, or non-communicative patients without immediate access to their vital medical history.

Our solution is to build a system where patients' basic medical records are safely stored and can be instantly accessed in emergencies, while also spreading awareness about existing medical schemes.
