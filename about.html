About Us

In India, medical emergencies are often complicated by a critical information gap at the point of care. First responders and emergency room staff frequently treat unconscious, disoriented, or non-communicative patients without immediate access to their vital medical history, such as known allergies, chronic conditions (like diabetes or hypertension), and current medications. This lack of readily available data leads to dangerous delays in diagnosis, increases the risk of life-threatening medical errors from adverse drug events, and ultimately compromises patient outcomes during the crucial "golden hour" of emergency care.

Our solution is to build a system where patients’ basic medical records are safely stored and can be instantly accessed in emergencies, while also spreading awareness about existing medical schemes so that people not only get faster treatment but also know how to use the healthcare support available to them.
